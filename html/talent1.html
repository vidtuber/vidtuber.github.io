Generation 1
H.O.T

Agency : SM Entertaiment

Created in 1996. They are the first K-pop idol group and they are so success.The group consisted of five members: Moon Hee-joon, Jang Woo-hyuk, Tony An, Kangta, and Lee Jae-won
Sechs Kies

Agency : YG Entertaiment

Making their debut on April 15, 1997, they are credited with pioneering idol fandom culture.The group currently consists of active members Eun Ji-won, Lee Jai-jin, Kim Jae-duck, and Jang Su-won.
S.E.S

Agency : SM Entertaiment

formed in 1997, with three members: Bada, Eugene, and Shoo. Their debut album I'm Your Girl sold 650,000 copies, becoming the second best-selling album by a female group in South Korea.
Fin.K.L

Agency : DSP Entertaiment

Lee Hyori, Ock Joo-hyun, Lee Jin, and Sung Yu-ri. Fin.K.L was one of the most popular K-pop groups of the late 1990s and early 2000s with released the songs "To My Boyfriend" in 1998.
Generation 2
Big Bang

Agency : YG Entertaiment

The group consists of four members: G-Dragon, T.O.P, Taeyang, and Daesung. Seungri departed in March 2019. Dubbed the "Kings of K-pop", they helped spread the Korean Wave internationally.
Super Junior

Agency : SM Entertaiment

Debuted on November 6, 2005, with the single "Twins (Knock Out)". Currently consists of 10 members, consisting of leader Leeteuk, Heechul, Yesung, Shindong, Sungmin, Eunhyuk, Donghae, Siwon, Ryeowook and Kyuhyun.
Girls Generation

Agency : SM Entertaiment

The group is composed of eight members: Taeyeon, Sunny, Tiffany, Hyoyeon, Yuri, Sooyoung, Yoona, and Seohyun. Originally a nine-piece ensemble, Jessica departed from the group in September 2014.
KARA

Agency : DSP Entertaiment

The group's final lineup was composed of Gyuri, Seungyeon, Hara and Youngji. Members Nicole and Jiyoung officially departed from the group in 2014, while Sunghee left in 2008.
Generation 3
BTS

Agency : Big Hit Entertaiment

Known as the Bangtan Boys, formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013. The septet—consisting of members Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook—co-writes and co-produces much of their own output.
EXO

Agency : SM Entertaiment

South Korean-Chinese boy band based in Seoul, consisting of nine members: Xiumin, Suho, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai and Sehun. The band was formed in 2011 and debuted in 2012
Tommorow x Together

Agency : Big Hit Entertaiment

Consists of five members Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun and HueningKai. They debuted on March 4, 2019 with the EP The Dream Chapter: Star
Red Velvet

Agency : SM Entertaiment

debuted on August 1, 2014, with the single "Happiness" with the four-member line-up of Irene, Seulgi, Wendy and Joy. A fifth member, Yeri, joined the group in March 2015
Twice

Agency : JYP Entertaiment

The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice was formed under the television program Sixteen (2015) and debuted on October 20, 2015
ITZY

Agency : JYP Entertaiment

members Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. The group debuted on February 12, 2019, with the release of their single album, It'z Different.
